ABOUT

Jeannie Han is a graphic designer based in New York/New Jersey.
She imagines design to be one of many ways to implement simplistic thinking into creating systems for complex/abstract ideas. The intersection of art and systems produces what is, to her, deemed functional and successful work. Her keen interests in art created through typography supports her ideas conceptually, while her passion for form–making creates multi–dimensional networks of interpretation.
