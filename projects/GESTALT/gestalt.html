The Gestalt Series

[Trading Card] [Identity]

The Gestalt Principles of art and design is a set of laws of human perception that helps us understand visually complex imagery and compositions in everyday life. Understanding these principles is incredibly essential in establishing hierarchy, clarity, and aesthetically pleasing and intuitive design.

The Gestalt Series is a collectible trading card identity that visually depicts 5 selected Gestalt Principles (Proximity, Continuity, Similarity, Closure, and Figure-Ground). Whether these cards are simply for collecting or serve as an important tool for learning, these cards are an ever-growing collectible series for creatives and the general consumer interested in art and aesthetics.
